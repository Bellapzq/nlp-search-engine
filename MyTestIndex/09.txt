Title: Travel Trends in 2024
Text: Travel has transformed over the decades, influenced by both societal 
changes and technological advancements. In 2024, sustainability has become a 
primary concern, with eco-tourism booming and travelers opting for environmentally 
friendly options. More people are choosing to travel locally or seeking 
immersive experiences that respect local cultures and ecosystems. "Travelers 
today want more than just sightseeing," says travel expert Rob C. "They want 
to make a positive impact." Isn't that a wonderful shift?
